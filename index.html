Why Is This Free?

The clinical trial data comes from ClinicalTrials.gov — a federal government database funded by your tax dollars. It's public information. We just made it usable.

Clinical trials are also free to participate in. Sponsors cover treatment costs, and many trials compensate participants for their time. You should never pay to join a clinical trial — if someone asks you to, it's a scam.

We keep this service free for patients. We're supported by the healthcare companies that benefit from finding the right patients for their trials.

What You Should Know Before You Search

🆓

Trials Are Free to Join

Sponsors pay for the experimental treatment. Many also compensate you for time and travel.

🚀

Access Cutting-Edge Treatments

Trials are often the only way to access treatments that won't be widely available for years.

👁️

Closely Monitored

Trial participants are watched more carefully than standard patients. More appointments, more data.

🚪

You Can Leave Anytime

Participation is always voluntary. You can withdraw from a trial at any time for any reason.
